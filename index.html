Most homeowners don't notice hail damage until a leak develops — sometimes months later. Signs include dented gutters, bruised or cracked shingles, and granule loss. The safest move is a free inspection from a qualified roofer after any major storm. We'll get up there and show you exactly what we find, no pressure.

If the damage was caused by a covered event — hail, wind, lightning — most homeowner policies will cover a full replacement minus your deductible. We work directly with adjusters every week and know how to document damage properly. We'll walk you through the entire process so you're not navigating it alone.

Most residential re-roofs are completed in a single day. Larger or more complex roofs may take two days. We set up before you wake up, work efficiently all day, and do a thorough cleanup — including magnetic nail sweeps — before we leave.

It's virtually impervious to hail that would total a shingle roof, and it can lower your insurance premiums and energy bills.
